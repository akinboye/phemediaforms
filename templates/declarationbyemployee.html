Undertakings & Declarations

1. Compliance with Company Rules and Regulations

I undertake to strictly obey and comply with all rules, regulations, policies, standing orders, instructions, and directives issued by Phemedia Onguard Services Ltd from time to time, including but not limited to the company's code of conduct, uniform policy, duty roster, reporting procedures, and any amendments thereto.

2. Punctuality and Attendance

I commit to being punctual for all assigned duties and shifts. Lateness, unauthorized absence, or absenteeism without prior approval is unacceptable and will attract disciplinary action, including warnings, salary deductions, suspension, or termination.

3. Maintenance of Good Character and Conduct

I pledge to maintain exemplary personal and professional conduct, high moral standards, integrity, honesty, discipline, and good behavior at all times. I shall not engage in any act that brings disrepute to the company, its clients, or the security profession.

4. Notice Period for Resignation / Termination

I agree to give a minimum of 30 (thirty) days' written notice to the management before resigning or terminating my employment. Failure to serve the required notice may result in forfeiture of any outstanding benefits, salary in lieu of notice deduction, or legal action for damages caused to the company.

5. Prohibition of Sabotage and Misconduct

I understand and agree that any form of sabotage, including but not limited to deliberate damage to company or client property, leakage of confidential information, negligence leading to security breach, insubordination, theft, fraud, collusion with unauthorized persons, or any act prejudicial to the company's interests/security operations, shall result in immediate dismissal without notice, forfeiture of all benefits, and possible legal prosecution under relevant Nigerian laws (including the Private Guard Companies Act and Criminal Code).

6. Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

I undertake to treat all company information, client details, operational strategies, security protocols, and any other sensitive data as strictly confidential during and after my employment. I shall not disclose, copy, or misuse such information without written permission.

7. Obedience to Lawful Orders and Assignment

I agree to perform all lawful duties assigned to me diligently, to the best of my ability, and in the interest of the company and its clients. I shall report any suspicious activity, incident, or irregularity immediately to my superior.

8. Uniform, Equipment, and Company Property

I undertake to wear the prescribed uniform properly, handle all company-issued equipment (e.g., baton, torch, radio, ID card) with care, and return the same in good condition upon leaving service. Any loss or damage due to negligence will be recovered from my salary/dues.
