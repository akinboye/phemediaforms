1. Purpose

The Client engages the Service Provider to conduct background check and related verification services on individuals, employees, contractors, or other persons as requested by the Client, in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

2. Scope of Services

The Service Provider shall provide background check including, but not limited to:

* Identity verification

* Criminal record checks (subject to legal requirements)

* Employment history verification

* Academic qualification verification

* Reference checks

* Credit checks (where legally permitted)

3. Client Responsibilities

The Client shall:

  • Provide accurate and complete information necessary for the Service Provider to carry out the checks
  • Obtain all necessary written consents from the subject(s) of the background check, as required by law
  • Use the information provided solely for lawful and legitimate purposes

4. Confidentiality

  • All information obtained, processed, or provided under this Agreement shall remain strictly confidential
  • Neither Party shall disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent, except where required by law
